Bitcoin Weekday Strategy Performance

Comparative analysis of single-weekday trading strategies versus buy-and-hold approach

Strategy Performance Hierarchy
Monday-only strategy demonstrates superior long-term performance, significantly outpacing buy-and-hold returns. Tuesday and Wednesday strategies also show competitive performance, while weekend strategies (Saturday/Sunday) generally underperform the market baseline.
Risk-Adjusted Returns
The cumulative return trajectories reveal that concentrated trading strategies can potentially deliver enhanced returns but with increased volatility exposure. The Monday strategy shows 3-4x outperformance versus buy-and-hold over the analysis period.
Market Timing Implications
Results suggest that systematic calendar effects in Bitcoin markets can be exploited through targeted exposure strategies. However, these strategies require precise execution and may not account for transaction costs and slippage in practical implementation.
